🏆 How Scoring Works

Predict the exact scoreline of each match. The closer you are, the more you score.

The Point Ladder

+10
Exact scoreBoth teams' goals correct (a perfect draw counts too)
+5
Right winner + goal differenceCorrect winner and margin, but not the exact score
+3
Right winner onlyCorrect team wins, but wrong margin
+3

0
Draw / win mix-upYou said draw but a team won — or you said a win but it drew
−3
Backed the wrong teamYou picked one side, the other side won

Everyone gets 2 Double Chips for the whole tournament. Play one on a match to double your points for it — the good and the bad.

Guru chips an exact 2–1 → +20. But Prajay chips a wrong-team pick → −6. Save them for matches you're confident about!
